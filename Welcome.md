# Launchers Research Vault

A research vault on Android (and adjacent) home-screen **launchers** — the most common ones, their pricing, features, and the mental models behind them.

👉 **Start at the [[Launchers MOC]]** (Map of Content).

## Structure
- **00 Home/** — the [[Launchers MOC]] hub.
- **Concepts/** — mental models, taxonomy, monetization, theming, privacy, glossary.
- **Launchers/** — one note per launcher.
- **Comparisons/** — [[Launcher Comparison Table]] and [[Pricing Comparison]].

> Tip: open the **graph view** to see how the concepts and launchers interlink. Prices/features were seeded as of 2026 and drift over time — verify before citing.

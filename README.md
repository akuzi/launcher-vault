# Launchers Research Vault

An [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) vault researching **Android (and adjacent) home-screen launchers** — the most common ones, their pricing, features, the mental models behind them, and how they grow and make money.

> Open the folder as a vault in Obsidian for the full experience: `[[wikilinks]]`, backlinks, and the graph view that shows how concepts and launchers interconnect.

## Structure

| Folder | Contents |
|---|---|
| `00 Home/` | **Launchers MOC** — the map-of-content hub linking everything |
| `Concepts/` | Mental models, taxonomy, profiles, theming, privacy, wellbeing, monetization, growth, glossary |
| `Launchers/` | One note per launcher (Nova, Niagara, Smart, Lawnchair, Olauncher, …) |
| `Comparisons/` | Side-by-side feature table and pricing comparison |

## Start here

- **Launchers MOC** (`00 Home/`) — the central index
- **What is a Launcher** — the replaceable home layer on Android
- **Launcher Mental Models** — the frames for reasoning about launchers
- **Launcher Comparison Table** / **Pricing Comparison** — the quick-reference views

## Topics covered

- **Concepts** — what a launcher is, mental models, taxonomy, [profiles](Concepts/Profiles.md) (launcher modes vs OS work/private spaces), icon packs & theming, gestures, privacy, digital wellbeing & minimalism, glossary
- **Business** — monetization models, growth & marketing strategies, growth case studies
- **Launchers** — power-user (Nova, Action, Smart, Total, Hyperion), minimalist (Niagara, Olauncher, Before), search-first (KISS), stock-like (Pixel, Lawnchair), ecosystem/info-dense (Microsoft, AIO), and OEM defaults

## A note on accuracy

Prices and feature details were seeded from general knowledge as of 2026 and **drift over time** (region, sales, version changes). Treat numbers as ballpark and verify before citing — the comparison notes carry the same caveat.

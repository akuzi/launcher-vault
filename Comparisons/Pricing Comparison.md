## Patterns to notice
- **FOSS and vendor-funded launchers are $0** but pay differently: FOSS in development pace ([[Monetization Models|bus-factor]]), vendor-funded in **data + ecosystem lock-in** ([[Privacy and Launchers]]).
- **Indie premium launchers** cluster around a **~$5 one-time** unlock — the historical sweet spot.
- **Subscription is rare and contested** ([[Niagara Launcher]] is the notable adopter) because users resent recurring fees for a set-and-forget surface (the core tension in [[Monetization Models]]).
- Almost everyone offers a **free tier** — the home screen is too central to gate entirely; paid tiers unlock *depth*, not *access*.

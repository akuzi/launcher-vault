# Lawnchair

An **open-source, Pixel-style** launcher: the clean Google look plus the customization the [[Pixel Launcher]] withholds. Built on AOSP's Launcher3.

## At a glance
- **Category**: [[Launcher Taxonomy|Stock-like / clean]]
- **Retrieval model**: spatial grid (see [[Launcher Mental Models]])
- **Developer**: Lawnchair community (open source)

## Pricing
- **Free**, no paid tier. Model: [[Monetization Models|FOSS]].

## Key features
- **Pixel-like UI** with At a Glance clone (Smartspace), search bar styling.
- **Material You / Monet** theming, themed icons (see [[Icon Packs and Theming]]).
- Icon pack support, **Lawnicons** companion (monochrome themed-icon set).
- Customizable grid, dock, drawer, gestures.
- No telemetry concerns — auditable source ([[Privacy and Launchers]]).

## Mental model fit
The "keep it stock but a bit better" branch of [[Launcher Taxonomy]]. Gives Pixel users the customization Google omits, and non-Pixel users the Pixel aesthetic — without the [[Privacy and Launchers|trust]] questions of closed launchers.

## Caveats
- As a community project, releases can be **beta-ish / slower** ([[Monetization Models|FOSS bus-factor]]).
- Tracks AOSP Launcher3, so some advanced power-user features lag [[Nova Launcher]].

## Best for
"Pixel look + customization, open source." Compare with [[Pixel Launcher]] (stock, locked down) and [[Nova Launcher]] (more power, closed source).

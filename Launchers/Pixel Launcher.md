# Pixel Launcher

Google's **stock launcher**, bundled with Pixel phones. The reference for "clean Android" and the source of the **At a Glance / Smartspace** pattern.

## At a glance
- **Category**: [[Launcher Taxonomy|Stock-like / clean]]
- **Retrieval model**: spatial grid + Google Search (see [[Launcher Mental Models]])
- **Developer**: Google

## Pricing
- **Free**, but **not officially installable** on non-Pixel devices (it depends on Pixel system components). Model: [[Monetization Models|vendor-funded]] — sells Pixel hardware and surfaces Google Search/Assistant.

## Key features
- **At a Glance** widget — contextual cards (next event, weather, travel, parking).
- Clean grid, bottom Google search/Assistant bar.
- Material You themed icons (see [[Icon Packs and Theming]]), wallpaper-driven palette.
- Tight integration with Google services and Pixel features.

## Limitations (by design)
- **Minimal customization** — no icon packs, limited grid/gesture control. Google keeps it simple deliberately.
- Pixel-locked. Users wanting the look elsewhere reach for [[Lawnchair]].

## Mental model fit
Pure "stock-like" point of the [[Launcher Mental Models|customization spectrum]], and a strategic vendor play like [[Microsoft Launcher]] (Google's services instead of Microsoft's).

## Best for
"I have a Pixel and want it the way Google intended." For the same look + freedom on any phone, see [[Lawnchair]].

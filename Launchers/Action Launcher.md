# Action Launcher

A power-user launcher known for **distinctive interaction concepts** (Covers, Shutters, Quickbar, Quicktheme) rather than just exposing every setting.

## At a glance
- **Category**: [[Launcher Taxonomy|Power-user / customization]]
- **Retrieval model**: spatial grid + gestures (see [[Launcher Mental Models]])
- **Developer**: Chris Lacy

## Pricing
- **Free** core; **Action Launcher Plus** one-time unlock (historically ~$4.99, sometimes via subscription bundle). See [[Pricing Comparison]].
- Model: [[Monetization Models|one-time unlock]].

## Signature concepts
- **Covers** — a folder that opens its primary app on tap, the folder contents on swipe (icon does double duty).
- **Shutters** — swipe an app icon to reveal its widget in place.
- **Quickbar / Quickpage** — a hidden side page you swipe in for extras.
- **Quicktheme** — auto-themes the UI from wallpaper colors (predated Material You; see [[Icon Packs and Theming]]).

## Mental model fit
A more **opinionated** take on the [[Launcher Mental Models|customization]] axis than [[Nova Launcher]]: instead of infinite knobs, it invents richer [[Gestures as Input|gesture]] interactions. Innovation-led.

## Best for
"I want clever interactions, not just more settings." Compare with [[Nova Launcher]] (breadth of settings) and [[Smart Launcher]] (automation).

# Before Launcher

A **minimalist, notification-centric** launcher. The home screen filters and surfaces only the notifications that matter, with a pared-back text-forward app list.

## At a glance
- **Category**: [[Launcher Taxonomy|Minimalist / intentional]]
- **Retrieval model**: short list + search (see [[Launcher Mental Models]])
- **Developer**: Before Labs

## Pricing
- **Free** core; small **premium** unlock for extras. See [[Pricing Comparison]].
- Model: [[Monetization Models|freemium / one-time]].

## Key features
- **Notification filtering** on the home screen — important alerts only, the rest muted/triaged.
- Minimal text-based app list, few colors (see [[Digital Wellbeing and Minimalism]]).
- Focus on calm, low-distraction daily use.

## Mental model fit
Minimalism via the **notification cue** angle rather than only the icon-cue angle — it tackles the *interruption* side of compulsive use (axis 2, [[Launcher Mental Models]]).

## Best for
"Notifications stress me out and I want a calmer screen." Compare with [[Olauncher]] (strips visuals) and [[Niagara Launcher]] (one-handed list).

# Hyperion Launcher

A **clean, modern** launcher from the team associated with the Substratum theming project. Sits between stock-like and power-user, with a polished aesthetic.

## At a glance
- **Category**: [[Launcher Taxonomy|Power-user / customization]]
- **Retrieval model**: spatial grid (see [[Launcher Mental Models]])
- **Developer**: Projekt / Substratum team

## Pricing
- **Free** core; **Supporter / unlock** as a one-time purchase for premium features. See [[Pricing Comparison]].
- Model: [[Monetization Models|one-time unlock]].

## Key features
- Modern, refined visual design with strong theming roots.
- Icon pack support, adaptive icons (see [[Icon Packs and Theming]]).
- Customizable grid, dock, drawer, gestures.
- At-a-Glance-style widget.

## Mental model fit
A middle-ground on the [[Launcher Mental Models|customization spectrum]]: more design polish than bare AOSP, less exhaustive than [[Nova Launcher]]. Appeals to users who weight aesthetics highly.

## Best for
"A good-looking, lightly powerful launcher from the theming community." Compare with [[Lawnchair]] (FOSS, stock) and [[Nova Launcher]] (more settings).

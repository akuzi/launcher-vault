# Nova Launcher

The long-standing **default recommendation for power users**. Mature, fast, deeply customizable, stock-like base.

## At a glance
- **Category**: [[Launcher Taxonomy|Power-user / customization]]
- **Retrieval model**: spatial grid (see [[Launcher Mental Models]])
- **Developer**: TeslaCoil Software (Kevin Barry)

## Pricing
- **Free** tier covers most layout customization.
- **Nova Prime** — one-time unlock, historically ~$4.99. Adds gestures, unread badges, hide apps, more. See [[Pricing Comparison]].
- Model: [[Monetization Models|one-time unlock]].

## Key features
- Highly customizable grid, icon size, labels, folders.
- Icon pack support (see [[Icon Packs and Theming]]).
- Extensive, remappable [[Gestures as Input|gestures]].
- App drawer customization (tabs, hide apps, custom drawer grid).
- Notification badges/dots.
- Backup & restore of full layout (lower switching cost).
- Subgrid positioning, custom app icons/labels.

## Mental model fit
The archetype of axis 3 ([[Launcher Mental Models|customization spectrum]]) at the "power-user" point: stock-like enough to feel native, but every parameter is exposed.

## Notable history / risk
Acquired by **Branch** (a mobile attribution/analytics company) in 2022, which triggered [[Privacy and Launchers|privacy]] concern and a period of slowed development and layoffs. Development later stabilised / spun back toward the original team. A textbook case of [[Monetization Models|launcher funding fragility]] and [[Launcher Mental Models|switching-cost lock-in]] biting users.

## Best for
"I want my home screen exactly my way, but it should still feel like Android." Compare with [[Action Launcher]] (more opinionated concepts) and [[Lawnchair]] (FOSS, more stock).

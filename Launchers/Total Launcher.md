# Total Launcher

A **build-from-scratch canvas** launcher. Rather than configuring presets, you place and design every element freely — closer to a UI editor than a settings screen.

## At a glance
- **Category**: [[Launcher Taxonomy|Power-user / build-from-scratch]]
- **Retrieval model**: fully custom (whatever you design) (see [[Launcher Mental Models]])
- **Developer**: SSLABS / Total Design team

## Pricing
- **Free** with limits; **one-time** key/unlock for full features. See [[Pricing Comparison]].
- Model: [[Monetization Models|one-time unlock]].

## Key features
- **Free-form placement** — no grid constraint; pixel-level positioning of items.
- Custom item types, overlapping elements, shapes, text, images.
- Deep theming and shared user-made themes/setups.
- Gestures and item actions.

## Mental model fit
The far "design every pixel" end of the [[Launcher Mental Models|customization spectrum]] (axis 3). Near-infinite control at high setup cost — kin to KLWP-style theming (see [[Icon Packs and Theming]]).

## Trade-offs
- **Steep effort**; not for users who want it working in five minutes.
- Many rely on **importing community setups** to skip the design work.

## Best for
"I want to design my home screen like a canvas." Compare with [[Nova Launcher]] (powerful but grid-based) and [[Action Launcher]] (concept-led).

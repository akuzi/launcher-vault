# Smart Launcher

Known for **auto-organising** apps into categories and **adaptive theming** that derives colors from the wallpaper, plus its signature **flower-shaped dock**.

## At a glance
- **Category**: [[Launcher Taxonomy|Adaptive / smart]]
- **Retrieval model**: categorised drawer + spatial (see [[Launcher Mental Models]])
- **Developer**: Smart Launcher Team (Italy)

## Pricing
- **Free** with ads/limited features.
- **Smart Launcher Pro** — historically a one-time purchase (~$8–15 region-dependent); also offered via subscription/bundles at points. See [[Pricing Comparison]].
- Model: mixed [[Monetization Models|one-time / subscription]].

## Key features
- **Automatic app categorisation** — apps sorted into tabs (Communication, Games, Media…) with no manual filing.
- **Adaptive colors** — UI palette generated from the wallpaper (Material You-like; see [[Icon Packs and Theming]]).
- **Flower dock** — distinctive curved arrangement of primary apps.
- Icon pack support, ambient theme, customizable grid.
- Gesture support, widget page, built-in weather.
- Multiple home configurations / modes (see [[Profiles]]).

## Mental model fit
Lands on the "organise it for me" decision branch ([[Launcher Taxonomy]]) — automation reduces setup cost while still offering power-user depth. Bridges adaptive convenience and customization.

## Best for
"I don't want to manually sort apps, and I like my UI to match my wallpaper." Compare with [[Nova Launcher]] (manual control) and [[Lawnchair]] (Material You done stock-style).
